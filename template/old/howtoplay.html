How to register your nick on Freenode?


Here are the steps to register a nick on Freenode, essential before registering into FLEQ. The steps are described using Webchat as Freenode IRC client, but you can use any other client to do it.

  1. Go to the Play section, fill in the Nickname field with the nick you want to register, write the two words you see written in captcha and press Connect.


  2. After accessing Freenode, execute on the bottom bar the command /msg nickserv info <nickname>, where you have to replace by the nickname/username you want to register.


  3. If after executing the command you receive a response saying that the nickname is not registered, you can continue to the next point.


    Use the command /nick <nick> to change your nickname to the nickname you have chosen the last step.


  4. The last step will be to execute the command /msg nickserv register <password> <email>, where you have to replace <password> and <email> by a password and a valid email address respectively.

  5. At this point you have registered your nickname on Freenode. Do not forget to use it both for playing the games and to register it in FLEQ.

How to register on FLEQ?


The only prerequisite before proceeding in this section is to have a registered your nick on Freenode, as explained in the previous section.

  1. The first step will be to access the register section.

  2. Now complete the fields of the form to create your account at FLEQ, and click the button to register.
    IMPORTANT INFO: Don't forget to select the same username/nickname that you registered on Freenode before.
  3. If all fields are correct, your account has been registered and you can access to FLEQ project to join and play games.

How to upload questions to the system?


After having logged in with your account into FLEQ, if your account have administrator privileges, you can upload questions to the system. Without questions, you cannot create a tournament.

  1. The first step will be to save the questions in a text file (use a text editor to do it, not a text processor but a text editor). Each line of that file will contain a question in the following format:
    category[,category]*;question;answer1;[answer2;[answer3;[answer4;]]]
    The brackets are optional fields and the asterisk means that the field can be repeated zero or many times.
    • Category: Indicates the categories of the question. At least should have one. Separate them by commas if they're more than one.
    • Question: Full text of the question.
    • Answer1: The correct answer to the question. This is the one that will be displayed to the players.
    • Answer2: It's optional and an alternative correct answer to the question. If included, part of it will be used to help the players as a hint (if configured by the administrator).
    • Answer3, answer4: They are also optional and alternative correct responses to the question.
    Example of the contents of a file with three questions:

    acertijos;¿De qué color era el caballo Blanco de Santiago?;Blanco;
    acrónimos,memes;¿Qué significa LOL?;Laughing Out Loud;Laugh Out Loud;Lots Of Laughs;
    arte,cultura general,personajes;¿Cuál era el nombre de la mujer del pintor Salvador Dalí?;Gala;Gala Dalí;Gala Eluard Dalí;Gala Éluard Dalí;
  2. To upload a question file select Admin Panel/Load Questions in the menu bar.


  3. To select the file you want to upload, you just have to select it in your file browser. The file should be formatted as defined above.


    You can repeat these steps as many times as you want to add more questions to the system.

How to create a tournament?


  1. Now that you know that the system has stored the questions in the category you want your tournament, you can create the tournament. To do this, select Admin Panel/New Tournament in the menu bar.


  2. Complete the fields of the form without leaving any empty and click Create New Tournament.


  3. If all fields are correctly completed, the tournament will be created and you can access it in section Tournaments / My Tournaments in the menu bar or in your sidebar. However, players can access to it in section Tournaments / My Tournaments in his menu bar.


How to join to a tournament?


If you already have a registered nickname on Freenode and have the same nickname in FLEQ, follow these steps to join a tournament.

  1. In section Login / Register, enter your username and password and click the Login button.


  2. In the menu bar, you can access all available tournaments. Click on Tournaments / Tournaments to see them.


  3. In the list of available tournaments, click on the (+ Join) button to join a tournament.


  4. If everything went OK, congratulations, from now you're a new player in this tournament. You can access to all information about your games in this tournament and table scores.


How to choose your start time preferences of a game?


Every time a new round begins, the system schedules the games. At this stage, you'll be able to change the default time at which the game will be play. Just follow these steps to learn how to choose the hour(s) you prefer:

  1. In your menu bar, in section Tournaments / My Next Games (or in your left sidebar as notification) you can see your next games. Click to go to this section to select your preferred hour and date options for each game.


  2. Now, you can select all starting hour and date combinations that you prefer to play the game. For example, you can select play at 4 PM on April 13, 2012 and click Save date and times to play. After that, you can select play at 10 PM on April 13, 2012 and click again Save date and times to play. And again, you can select play at 16 PM on April 14, 2012. All combinations are saved.


  3. After your preferences have been saved, the system will check the preferences of your opponent. If any of them matches the ones chosen by your opponent, the starting time of the game is modified. You will be informed via email about this change.


How to access Freenode using Webchat?


Webchat is a quick and easy way to connect to the Freenode IRC network without havingn to install any additional software. You even don't have to leave the FLEQ website for doing this.
If you want to use a local client installed in your computer, chech the FAQ section to see different alternatives and how config them.

The steps to access using the nickname and password obtained in the previous sections are as follows.

  1. In Webchat main screen, complete the fields: Nickname, Username (the same that Nickname) and Password before clicking the connect button.


  2. If the data is correct, you are connected to the Freenode IRC network and you've authenticated your nickname and your password.

How to play a game?


This section explains step by step how to access a scheduled game.
Although Webchat has been used to illustrate these examples, the steps are not very different if you use any other IRC client.

  1. The first step to play the game for the round that you have to play is to access the Freenode IRC network using your previously registered nickname. You have the details of how to do it in the previous section.
    IMPORTANT: remember you have to be logged in with your nickname at least 5 minutes before of the start time of the game.
  2. Once you've logged in, watch the content to the tab Status, because that is where you will receive an invitation to join the channel where the game will be played.


  3. You can accept this invitation and join the channel in two ways:

    • Clicking the name of the channel that you have been invited.


    • Running the command /join #<channel>, replacing <channel> by the name of the channel that you have been invited to.


  4. Once in the channel where the game takes place, you'll have to wait until the starting time. When the game starts, FLEQbot will begin to ask questions and you'll have to answer correctly before your opponent to get a point.

How to request a review of a question in a game?


Below are the steps to follow for any player who wants to claim a review of a question asked in a game.

  1. After a game ends, you can access the details of it through the tournaments site (remember: Tournaments / My Tournaments in your menubar).



  2. In the details of the game you will see a link to request a review of a question.

  3. On the next screen you'll have to write the Question Number (which appears at the beginning of each question during the IRC game) and the text you want to attach to the review.


  4. After submitting it, your review request will be sent to the administrator of the tournament who will consider the revision. You will be notified via e-mail when an answer has been given to your request. Then, following paragraph 1 of this section, you will see the response provided.
